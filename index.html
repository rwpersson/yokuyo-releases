Yokuyo 抑揚

See the melody of Japanese.

Yokuyo visualizes sentence-level pitch accent — the continuous rise and fall of real intonation across a whole phrase, not just word-by-word dictionary lookups.

Download for Windows

Free · Windows 10 / 11

What it does

Sentence-level pitch contour

See the continuous F0 curve for any Japanese sentence — how accent phrases connect and where downstep happens.

OJAD-style notation

Clear high/low step marks above the kana, with the original text aligned beneath each phrase.

Listen & shadow

Hear the sentence with natural speech synthesis, adjust speed, and loop phrases for practice.

Pitch accent rules

Tap any word to see the pitch accent rules for its word class — nouns, verbs, adjectives, particles, and more.

In your browser

Companion browser extension

Hold Shift and hover over Japanese text on any web page to see its pitch contour, right alongside your popup dictionary. The extension works together with the desktop app running on your computer. (Coming soon to the Chrome Web Store.)
